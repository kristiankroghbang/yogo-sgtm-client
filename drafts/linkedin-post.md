If you run a yoga studio or fitness business on YOGO Booking, you have probably struggled with tracking conversions.

The booking flow runs in an iframe. Client-side GTM cannot reach it. Ad blockers kill your tracking pixels. You are flying blind on ad spend.

YOGO recently released a proper REST API - and I built an open-source server-side GTM integration for it.

It polls the YOGO API for new orders, bookings, and customer signups, then sends them straight to your sGTM container. From there you can fire GA4, Meta CAPI, Google Ads conversions - whatever you need.

What it gives you:
- 100% accurate conversion data (directly from YOGO's database, not client-side scripts)
- Purchase revenue in GA4 and ad platforms
- Booking and check-in analytics
- Remarketing audiences based on actual behavior
- New customer data piped to your CRM

Zero dependencies. MIT licensed. Full setup guide included.

GitHub: https://github.com/kristiankroghbang/yogo-sgtm-client
Blog post: [link to kristiankroghbang.com post]

Shout out to the YOGO team for finally shipping the API - this would not have been possible without it.

Built with Claude 4.6 (Anthropic).
